How does your balance work?

Your balance are pre-paid funds you have available to spend on your mentoring sessions.

Your available balance will be used to pay for your mentoring sessions before any other stored payment method is used.

You can add money to your balance through the top-up function or through an employer.

Funds added to your balance grant you a non-transferrable license for services on MentorCruise and are therefore non-refundable and expire a year after purchase.
